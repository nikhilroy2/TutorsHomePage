Five characteristics of an Effective Trainer

 

Empathetic

Understands what it is like to be a student who may lack confidence,feel stressed, be overwhelmed or just not understand the material.

Asks good, open-ended questions

The tutor should ask the student questions in order to evaluate a students understanding.

 
 

Enthusiastic

If the tutor does not display a love for the subject they are teaching, how can they expect the student to enjoy it. Come to the classroom with a positive attitude that wil rub off on the students.

Patient

One of the most important characteristics of a tutor. Never act annoyed that the students does not know something. Even if they ask the most basic question, always demonstrate your patience.

 
 

Motivator

Encourages students to strive to be the best they can, to reach their goals, to recognize their strengths, to focus on learning how to learn so that they will be successful in the future.
